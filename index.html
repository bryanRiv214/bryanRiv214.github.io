Picture Puzzle

Picture Puzzle is a project I completed during my Junior year at Brooklyn Technical High School. The premise of Picture Puzzle is that the user must unscramble a picture by swapping two pictures at a time and achieve the lowest score possible. The program utilizes HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This project was extremely meaningful as it taught me how to manipulate the elements of a wesbite.
